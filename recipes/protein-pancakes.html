Gluten Free Protein Pancakes

Quick and easy recipe, but gives you a healthy and simple way to up your protein intake.

It is great topped with berries.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 7 egg whites
  • 1 drop vanilla extract
  • 1 serving cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Blend cottage cheese, oats, egg whites, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Spray a griddle or skillet with cooking spray and place over medium heat.
  3. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.

Chocolate Protein Iced Coffee

Made with coffee ice cubes, this smoothie is packed with protein from soy milk, spinach, and protein powder.

The frozen banana adds creaminess.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 cup coffee ice cubes
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder

Steps:

  1. Put soy milk in blender
  2. Put ice coffee cubes in blender
  3. Put spinach in blender
  4. Put banana in blender
  5. Put protein powder in blender
  6. Blend it
